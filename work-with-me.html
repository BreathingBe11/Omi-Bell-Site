Book Speaking

Omi Bell brings over a decade of lived experience as a founder, builder, and wellness practitioner to every stage. Her talks don't deliver information—they shift perspective. Audiences leave with new frameworks for how they think about leadership, wellbeing, and what it means to build something sustainable.

Body, Business & Belonging

The signature talk exploring integration over compartmentalization—why high performers perform better when they stop treating their work and their wellness as separate departments.

The Architecture of Belonging

For organizations building culture. How leaders create spaces where people can show up fully, contribute meaningfully, and stay.

Sustainable Success

For entrepreneurship conferences and business events. What it actually looks like to build something lasting without burning yourself down in the process.

Ideal For

Conferences, corporate leadership events, entrepreneurship summits, university programs, and organizations investing in culture, belonging, and sustainable performance.

Book Experience

Omi is a certified Yoga Nidra practitioner, breathwork facilitator, and sound healer. Her wellness experiences are designed specifically for leaders, founders, and high performers who have forgotten what it feels like to be inside their own body. These are not passive spa days—they are active, somatic practices with measurable effect.

Breathwork

Guided breathwork sessions for groups ranging from corporate teams to retreat cohorts. Accessible, powerful, and regulation-focused.

Yoga Nidra

The practice of yogic sleep. A guided state of conscious rest that restores the nervous system at a level that regular sleep often can't reach.

Retreats & Sound Healing

Immersive multi-modality retreat experiences. Sound baths, breathwork, and somatic practices woven together into a full-day or multi-day journey.

Ideal For

Corporate wellness programs, team retreats, leadership offsites, wellness conferences, festivals, and organizations investing in the nervous system health of their people.

Work Together

For founders, executives, and organizations who need more than inspiration—they need architecture. Omi brings over a decade of hard-won experience building an organization from nothing, combined with the operational clarity that comes from having rebuilt from the ground up. This work creates systems that don't depend on heroics.

Smooth Operator

A structured advisory program for founders who are operationally overwhelmed. We build systems, decision-making frameworks, and sustainable operating models.

Founder Advising

Ongoing thought partnership for founders navigating growth, transitions, or complexity. Someone who has built to ask the questions you haven't thought to ask yet.

Leadership Systems

For organizations building leadership infrastructure. Decision-making clarity, delegation frameworks, and the operational architecture of a team that functions without everything routing through one person.

Ideal For

Founders in growth stages, executive leaders navigating scale, and organizations that have outgrown their current operating model and need someone who has been there.
