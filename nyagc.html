Why This Exists

This is where two or three gather and agree.

Most group chats are full of noise. NYAGC is an energy container — a place where every member is actively pulling their future into their present. Here, you don't wait for the life you want. You speak it, claim it, and hold each other accountable to the vision that already exists in the unseen.

This is not a motivational space. It's not a hype house. It's a discipline — a weekly ritual of speaking life into what you are building, and being witnessed by people who believe it alongside you.

The Weekly Practice

What is The Decree?

Every week, each member drops their Decree — a present-tense, future-forward declaration of their life as it is already becoming. You are not wishing. You are not hoping. You are speaking what is already real in the frequency of where you are headed.

The language shifts from will to is. From I hope to have to I have. Your Decree speaks the life you are pulling into view as though it already exists — because energetically, it does.

Decree Language — Examples

  • I am leading a team that trusts my vision and moves with me.
  • I have the revenue, the time, and the clarity to build what matters.
  • In the next 30 days, I am closing a deal that changes the trajectory of my business.
  • In the next 90 days, I am living in full alignment with everything I have been building toward.
  • Tomorrow, I am showing up as the most grounded version of myself.
  • I am not becoming. I already am.

Your Decree can be a paragraph or a list. It can cover your business, your body, your relationships, your peace — whatever you are actively summoning. Speak it like it has already landed.

The Container Has Rules

How NYAGC Works

We begin on Tuesday, June 2, 2026 — the first Tuesday of June. From that point forward, Tuesdays are sacred.

  • 01

    Post Your Decree Every Tuesday

    Every Tuesday, you drop your Decree in the group. Present tense. Future-forward. Witnessed by everyone in the container.

  • 02

    Thursday is Your Grace Period

    If Tuesday doesn't work, you have until end of day Thursday. Life happens — but the commitment must be honored within the window.

  • 03

    No Post by Thursday = Removed

    If Thursday passes without your Decree, you will be removed from the group. This is not personal. The container only works when everyone is actively in it.

  • 04

    You Can Request to Return

    If you're removed and want back in, reach out. You'll be asked to re-commit. Once re-added, you have 24 hours to drop your Decree — or the door closes permanently.

  • 05

    Witness, Affirm, Agree

    When others post, read it. Respond with agreement. Speak back to them what you see. Your engagement is part of the practice — this is a two-and-three-touching-and-agreeing space.

  • 06

    July 2nd — We Regroup

    This is a one-month experiment. On July 2, 2026, we pause together to assess. What did the container produce? What did we feel? We decide as a group whether NYAGC continues.
