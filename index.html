About Omi

This isn't a
reinvention.
It's a return.

I built Black Girl Ventures from nothing. A teepee in my living room. T-shirts. Marbles in coffee cups as pitch judges. I ran hard for a decade. And in 2022, I hit a wall that a week off couldn't fix. A month off couldn't fix. I needed something deeper.

A three-month sabbatical and a deep dive into nervous system regulation later, I realized I had always been a healer. I just needed to rest long enough to remember.

Now I create spaces where leaders, founders, and high performers can stop long enough to hear themselves think again. To feel what their body has been trying to tell them. To rest on purpose, not by accident.
